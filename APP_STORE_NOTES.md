Promotional description:
Train your throw, shape your session ritual, and discover the cultural identity of tejo through a premium mobile experience.

Full description:
Mel Tejo is a premium sports app and mini-game inspired by tejo, the iconic Colombian throwing sport. It combines skill-based play, precision training, session challenges, and in-app cultural reading into one distinctive experience.

Instead of copying a generic sports dashboard, Mel Tejo is built around the feeling of the throw itself:
- Train power, arc, and composure in Throw Lab
- Complete ritual-based progress in Session Deck
- Read original in-app Atlas articles about the sport
- Track your long-term player identity in Signature
